Arrival and accommodation

Oulu is easily accessible by plane. Oulu Airport is the second busiest airport in Finland and dozens of flights from Finland as well as abroad arrive there on daily basis.

For travellers from closer areas, travelling by bus or train is ideal; you can get to Oulu from almost everywhere in Finland in a short period of time. The Oulu region is ideally covered with a rental car, while moving around in Oulu is convenient by bicycle or by local bus.

Events 2020

Several large international events take place in Oulu annually. The most popular ones are Qstock Festival, which attracts more than 60 different artist and 30 000 visitors, and Air Guitar World Championships, where contestants from all over the world take the stage to show what they can do with their imaginary instruments.
