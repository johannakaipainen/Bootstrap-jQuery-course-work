1. What is the capital city of Vietnam?

Hanoi is the capital of Vietnam. Ho Chi Minh is tha largest city and it was the capital of South Vietnam in 1954 - 1976. Saigon is Ho Chi Minh City's old name.

2. What is the capital city of Pakistan

Islamabad is the capital of Pakistan. Kabul is the capital of Afghanistan. Lahore is the second largest city of Pakistan.

3. What is the capital city of Nigeria?

Abuja is the capital of Nigeria and only the sixth largest city in the country. Lagos is the largest city of Nigeria. Accra is the capital city of Ghana.

4. What is the capital city of Uruguay?

Montevideo is the capital and the largest city of Uruguay. According to a study the quality of life in Montevideo is the best in Latin America. Buenos Aires is the capital of Argentina and Asuncion the capital of Paraguay.
